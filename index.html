FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

What services does Blumotech offer?

We build websites, create graphic design and brand assets, and plan advertising campaigns. We also develop our own apps and web platforms.

How do we start a project?

Send a message through the contact form or email sales@blumotech.com. We'll schedule a short call to understand your goals, timeline, and budget.

Do you work with clients outside Canada?

Yes. We're based in Toronto, Ontario, and work with clients remotely — including international projects such as a law firm website in Ukraine (gp.od.ua).

Can you redesign an existing site or only build new ones?

Both. We can refresh branding, rebuild on modern stacks, or improve UX and performance on what you already have.
